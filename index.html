About the Manasota Sax Quartet
The Manasota Saxophone Quartet was formed in 2004. They specialize in rag time, classical, jazz, holiday, and popular music. They have performed at many Assisted Living facilities throughout the Bradenton/Sarasota area, the Metro Cafe in Sarasota, the Wyland Art Gallery, the Anna Maria Island Flower Tour, and other similar venues.
